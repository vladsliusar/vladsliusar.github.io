Vladimir Sliusar

Creative and passionate GIS developer and web cartographer.




Hello, I’m Vladimir Sliusar, I go by Vlad. I’m a driven GIS professional with several years of experience in spatial data analysis, GIS scripting, web map creation, and geospatial database design, operations and implementation.

I received my BA in Geography at San Francisco State University and am currently pursuing a Master’s in GIS Development from the University of Wisconsin-Madison.

My educational and professional experiences have allowed me to strengthen my passion towards the field of GIS and Web Cartography. More specifically, I’m interested in applying my programming, design, and GIS skills towards solving various geospatial problems.

Aside from my GIS experience, I have spent numerous years working in luxury hospitality. Although I never saw it as my career, I greatly appreciate the opportunity as it has provided me with valuable customer service skills and allowed me to create lasting relationships with co-workers and clients alike.
